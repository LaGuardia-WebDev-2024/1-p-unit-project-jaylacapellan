COOKIES AND BROWNIES

Cookies and brownies are like a warm hug of goey, rich goodness. Cookies come in so many different varieties that you can just never get enough. Some of those are;

  • chocolate chip
  • sugar cookies
  • smores cookies(not my personal fav)
  • snickerdoodle
  • oatmeal raisn

and so many more. And the best part about cookies is your can get them crispy and crunchy or warm and soft. Brownies are rich and full of chocolatey goodness. You can get cakey or fudgy and know that it will be good either way especially if it has the little crackles on the top.

CAKES

Cakes can come in so many different shapes, sizes, flavors and colors. You can really customize them to be whatever you want so the only way you could ever go wrong is with cheesecake. Some of the many cake flavors you can get are;

  • chocolate
  • vanilla
  • red velvet
  • confetti
  • carrot cake
  • coffee cake
  • tiramisu
  • fruit cake
  • tres leches
  • dulce de leche

and obviously many more. Best part about cakes are if you don't want the whole cake,just get a cupcake!

ICECREAM

On a scorching hot, sunny day and matter of fact even on a cold, snowy day I can and will always enjoy a cup of cold, soft, refreshing icecream. You can choose to enjoy your icecream in a cup, sugar cone, or waffle cone and can even add whatever toppings you want such as candy, popping boba, fruit, syrups or whatever you want. My personal favorites are sprinkles, brownies, cookie dough, and nutella. However, I only get toppings with froyo. Some of my favorite icecream flavors are salted caramel,(hot-take) mint-chocolate chip, chocolate chip cookie dough and of course an original cookies and cream. I would honestly eat almost any icecream flavor.
